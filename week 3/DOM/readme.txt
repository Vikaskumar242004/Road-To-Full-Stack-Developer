1. What is DOM ?

  Fetching elements 
    ther are 5 popular methods available for fetching DOM elements :-

       - querySelector
       - querySelectorAll
       - getElementById
       - getElementByClassName
       - getElementsByClassName



Why DOM ?

   The DOM abstracts the structure of the document into a tree of objects, allowing scripts
   to manipulate thee content and structure dynamically. this abstraction enables more
   complex interactions and functionalities beyond just static HTML.
